## 2025Chronicle

### new house

On the night of moving, put the small lamp on the ground，There are still boxes piled up in the house。We use cardboard boxes as tables，Had a takeaway。When I woke up the next day, I felt like I had officially moved in.。

### A trip to a small town

I temporarily decided to go to a seaside town，Arrival early in the morning，I ate hot soy milk in front of the station first。Walk along the embankment，The sea breeze blew the hat around。Put the ticket in your wallet when you come back。

### Return at night

Go back to your usual intersection at night，The store name has been changed twice。The rain just stopped，Reflective road surface。He stood for a while before leaving。

### used bookstore

Hiding in a used bookstore when it rains，The little bell at the door keeps ringing。I picked up a second-hand prose book，I read a few pages in the store。The boss wraps books in old newspapers。

### Candlelight rice

a power outage，Eat with candles。The dishes are very simple，The house was so quiet that I could hear the rain。Later, I added the spare candles。

### Photo frame photo

When organizing photo albums, pick two old photos and put them in the same frame。Put it by the window，The light is just right in the morning。When you pass by, you’ll take a second look。

### Reunited after a long separation

A friend I haven't seen for many years comes to visit.，Take a walk to the river together。Chat till late，There's still a lot of words left unfinished。Before leaving, make an appointment not to be too long next time。

### Running at night

Start running at night after it gets colder，Fixed a long street。The street lights pass by one by one，The footsteps are very clear。Have a good sleep after returning home。

> Nothing big happened this year，But there are many memorable moments left behind。
